Dealing with conflicts




Communication styles that cause conflicts

Extreme statements: "It just must be done according to my will," "He always misbehaves with me," "He never agrees with me," and "You are all the same."

Fortune teller's mistake: "Whatever I do, he will never listen to me" and "She will not agree with me because she doesn't like me."

Emotional arguments: "I know he will do this," "You have a point, but I am not going to change my decision."

Mixing positive with negative: You combine the positive with negative, e.g., saying to your wife "You are beautiful, but you don't know how to dress."

Personal rules/principles: "Everyone should agree with me," "They should respect me & obey my rules."
