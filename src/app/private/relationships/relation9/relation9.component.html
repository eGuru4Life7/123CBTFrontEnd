How to say no?

People often find it difficult to say no for a variety of reasons. Saying yes to others out of politeness can be a cause of stress in our cultures. Usually saying “No” politely but firmly is enough.

However, if it does not work do the followings:

1. Summarise other person's request, so they know you listened to them

2. Explain the reason for saying “No.”

3. If you find the above two steps difficult you can delay your response by saying “Let me check my schedule, and I'll get back to you”.

4. You can always suggest an alternative, e.g., we can't meet tonight because I am busy, what about the next week?
