What are Emotions?

Emotions are those feelings that occur due to an event. They are often linked with your thoughts. Emotions are also called “mood”. Emotions can be both positive and negative. Whenever something happens, you think about it, and often those thoughts lead to certain emotions. We often link our emotions with bodily sensations, e.g., my head became hot with anger, my heart is sad, his gall bladder turned into water due to fear.

How would you feel if you were in one of the following situations?

The death of a relative or loved one

Someone scolds you

You lost your job

You are caught while stealing something from a shop
