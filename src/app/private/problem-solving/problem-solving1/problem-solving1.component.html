Problem-solving

Everyone faces problems from time to time. People often can solve problems quickly or with the help of friends. However, sometimes problems can be overwhelming, and people find it difficult to cope with them. The relationship between stress and problems is not a straightforward one. Sometimes people can become stressed because they had too many problems. But a stressed person might find even ordinary problems difficult to cope with.

People usually experience problems in these areas:

Family life (relationships, housing, children, debts etc.)

Social life

Work (problems at work, relationship with colleagues, stress at work, or even loss of work)
