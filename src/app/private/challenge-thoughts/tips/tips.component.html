Tips for challenging your thinking errors

Some people find it difficult to evidence against their thoughts. So try to use the second dairy with a friend or family member. After some practice you will start thinking against your thoughts. From now on whenever you experience negative emotion, ask youself wheather it is a thinking error and think about the evidence that are in favor of or against it.

The following questions can help you in challenging your thoughts.

What am I thinking? Is this a thinking error?

Is there evidence for this thought?

What is the evidence against this thought?

What is the evidence against this thought?
