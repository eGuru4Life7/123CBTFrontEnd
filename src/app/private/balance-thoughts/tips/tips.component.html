Tips for creating a balanced thought

Unlike the cognitive errors that first come to your mind, the alternative thought is more likely to be realistic & balanced. Sometimes it helps if you ask a friend, family member or a neighbour to help you, at least at the start.

These questions can help you in thinking of alternative thoughts.

What would a family member or a friend think when in a similar situations?

What would have I thought if this had happened in the past, or when I was not so stressed?

What would I think if this happens in future when I am not so stressed?

If my friend was thinking as this what would have, I said?
