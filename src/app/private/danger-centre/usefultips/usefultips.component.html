Some tips for dealing with stress

Spiritual & religious practices: Spiritual and religious practices can help with your stress levels.

Imagery: You can also reduce stress with the help of imagining beautiful places and pleasant events and things.

Massage: It is common in our culture for people to get a head and neck massage. It can help with stress and anxiety .

Distraction: Talking to someone, religious activities (for example, saying your prayers, reciting Quran or using beads), reading, watching TV, listening to the radio and physical activities.

Change in lifestyle: a healthy lifestyle and regular exercise help. Being very competitive, pessimistic, controlling, perfectionism, narcissism can all lead to stress, anxiety & depression.
